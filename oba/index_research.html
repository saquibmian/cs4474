Research Interest
My main area of research is the design and analysis of algorithms. Most of my research has centered on the design of approximation algorithms. However, I am interested also in the design of efficient data structures, parallel algorithms, distributed algorithms, and randomized algorithms.

Application areas from where most of the problems that I have studied come from are: networks, web caching and web searching, Computationa Biology, and data mining.

Recent work has included:
  • Algorithms for web related problems.
  • Approximation algorithms for network problems.
  • Approximation algorithms for scheduling problems.
  • Robustness analysis for optimization problems.
